David Chelimsky and I were chatting a couple of weeks ago about my recently acquired running hobby and he asked “Why don’t you organize a 5k race at RubyConf?” Then I said something like “heh”. Then we both thought about it some more and thought it might actually be a fun thing to do. In a previous email, Jarkko Laine had volunteered to coordinate some morning exercise sessions at RubyConf, so we’ve pulled him in as well.
